FAQ'S

Is your pay Monthly Membership contract?

Our Pay monthly membership has a fixed term of 3 months. A member can terminate their pay monthly online on their Fitfox members account after their first 3 months.

Are refunds available?

As per our club's terms and conditions, we have a no refund policy.

Can i get personal training?

Yes. Personalised programmes are available for better transformation with personal tranings.

I have a health issue, can i still exercise?

Yes, but it will depend on your condition and medical clearance may need to be provided by your doctor.If you have a specific medical issue, please email the details in confidence to fitfoxstudio@gmail.com and attach a copy of your doctor/physio clearance letter to this email.

Are there any washrooms and changing rooms available?

Yes we have seperate male and female changing rooms and washrooms.
